Roland 
  • Species: Reindeer
  • Gender: Male (he/him)
  • Age: 22
  • Sexuality: Gay

Roland is a Christmas-obsessed reindeer. During the month of december he's a bundle of joy (and sticks, lol), but during the other 11 months of the year, he's far more reserved and dresses with a much more reserved, darker look.

He's a photographer and a major tech nerd, some of his interests include amateur radio and signal identification. He's the type of guy who sleeps to a UVB-76 feed every night.

He was my main fursona for about one month, in december 2023, but I just couldn't make him work in the non-holiday months, so he ended up getting shelved pretty quick, even for my standards.

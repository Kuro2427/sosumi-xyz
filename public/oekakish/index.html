About

Welcome to /oekakish/! This is one of those anonymous drawing submission boards, like the ones kids these days have on their "straw paiges" or whatever. Just doodle something, hit submit and it should pop up on the gallery below. Pretty nifty, right?

F.A.Q

Q: What's with the weird name?

A: This page is called oekakish because it's not quite an oekaki!

Q: Why is the canvas so small?

To both save on bandwidth and also because I just plain like the more pixelated look 256x256 has compared to 512x512. It's worth noting, though, that this page is set up so if you zoom in the canvas should remain pixelated.

Q: This page is broken on mobile!

A: Are you new here?
